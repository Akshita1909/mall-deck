# Dubai Mall Interactive Sales Deck

## Overview
This project is an interactive, browser-based sales deck built around Dubai Mall. The idea was to move away from static presentations and create something more engaging, visual, and easy to explore.

Instead of relying on PDFs or manual explanations, this prototype allows a user to understand the scale, positioning, and business potential of the property on their own.

It is designed for potential tenants, brand partners, and event organizers who are evaluating whether to invest in a presence at the mall.

---

## What the project focuses on

The main goal was to build a simple but effective experience that:
- Grabs attention quickly through a strong visual entry point
- Communicates key information in a clear and structured way
- Highlights both the commercial and experiential aspects of the property
- Encourages action, such as partnerships or leasing discussions

---

## Features

- A full-screen, video-based landing section to set the tone
- A “Why this property” section with key metrics
- Sections covering attractions and retail presence
- A simple call-to-action aimed at partnerships and business opportunities
- Basic animations to make interactions feel smoother and more modern

---

## Tech Stack

- React (with Vite)
- Framer Motion for animations
- CSS for styling

---

## Approach

I focused on keeping the design clean and straightforward rather than overloading it with features. The idea was to create something that feels modern and easy to navigate, while still communicating the right message.

The structure is intentionally simple, but it is built in a way that can be expanded later into more detailed modules such as events, sponsorships, or leasing flows.

---

## Use of AI

AI tools were mainly used to speed up ideation and help with structuring the content and layout. The final decisions around design and implementation were kept practical and focused on clarity.
